# Best Passive Income Websites

Websites remain one of the best long-term passive income assets.

## Blogging Websites

Generate traffic through SEO and monetize with ads.

## Affiliate Websites

Promote products and earn commissions.

## Niche Tools Websites

Provide calculators and utilities that attract search traffic.

## Educational Websites

Offer guides, tutorials, and digital products.

## Membership Sites

Provide premium content for subscribers.

## Final Thoughts

The best passive income websites combine useful content with multiple monetization methods.
