# TikTok Money Calculator Guide

TikTok money calculators estimate earnings using RPM and view counts.

## Basic Formula

Revenue = Views × RPM ÷ 1000

## Example

* 500,000 views
* $2 RPM

Estimated earnings:

$1,000

## Why Calculators Are Useful

They help creators:

* Forecast revenue
* Compare niches
* Plan monetization strategies

## Final Thoughts

Money calculators provide useful estimates but actual earnings vary.
