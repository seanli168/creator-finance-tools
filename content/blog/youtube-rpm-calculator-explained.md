# YouTube RPM Calculator Explained

YouTube RPM calculators estimate earnings based on views and RPM.

## Formula

Revenue = Views × RPM ÷ 1000

## Example

* Views: 1,000,000
* RPM: $5

Estimated revenue: $5,000

## Why RPM Varies

* Audience location
* Niche type
* Video length
* Engagement rate

## Final Thoughts

RPM calculators are useful for estimates but not exact predictions.
