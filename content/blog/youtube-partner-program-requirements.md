# YouTube Partner Program Requirements

The YouTube Partner Program (YPP) allows creators to earn money from their videos.

## Basic Requirements

Most creators must meet:

* 1,000 subscribers
* 4,000 public watch hours

or

* 10 million Shorts views

## Additional Eligibility

Your channel must:

* Follow YouTube monetization policies
* Be located in a supported country
* Have an active AdSense account

## Benefits Of Joining YPP

Creators gain access to:

* Ad revenue
* Channel memberships
* Super Thanks
* Shopping features

## Final Thoughts

The YouTube Partner Program remains the primary path to earning ad revenue on YouTube.
