# How Much Does TikTok Pay Per 1000 Views?

TikTok does not pay a fixed rate per 1000 views. Earnings depend on several factors:

## TikTok RPM Range

Most creators earn:

* $0.02 – $0.10 per 1000 views (low monetization regions)
* $0.10 – $0.40 per 1000 views (average regions)
* $0.50 – $1.00+ per 1000 views (high-value niches)

## What Affects TikTok Pay?

### 1. Country of Audience

US / UK / Canada pay much higher RPM.

### 2. Niche

Finance, business, and tech = higher RPM
Entertainment = lower RPM

### 3. Monetization Program

TikTok Creator Fund vs Creativity Program changes payouts significantly.

## Example Calculation

If RPM = $0.20:

* 100,000 views → $20
* 1,000,000 views → $200

## Why TikTok Pays So Low?

TikTok monetization is still early-stage compared to YouTube:

* Lower ad revenue share
* Short-form content = lower ad value

## Conclusion

TikTok earnings vary widely, but most creators should expect:

👉 $20 – $200 per 1M views depending on niche and region
