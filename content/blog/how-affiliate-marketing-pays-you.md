# How Affiliate Marketing Pays You

Affiliate marketing rewards publishers for referring customers.

## Common Payment Models

### Pay Per Sale

The most common model.

### Pay Per Lead

Earn when users complete forms or sign up.

### Recurring Commissions

Receive ongoing payments from subscriptions.

## Payment Methods

Many affiliate programs pay via:

* PayPal
* Bank transfer
* Payoneer

## Payment Thresholds

Programs often require:

* $50 minimum
* $100 minimum

before issuing payments.

## Final Thoughts

Affiliate marketing payments vary by program but generally reward performance-based referrals.
