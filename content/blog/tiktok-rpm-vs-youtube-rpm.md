# TikTok RPM vs YouTube RPM

Creators often compare TikTok and YouTube earnings.

## Average RPM Comparison

| Platform | RPM Range   |
| -------- | ----------- |
| TikTok   | $0.20 - $10 |
| YouTube  | $1 - $20+   |

## Why YouTube Often Pays More

* Longer watch time
* More ad inventory
* Better advertiser targeting

## Why TikTok Still Matters

* Faster growth
* Easier virality
* Strong sponsorship opportunities

## Conclusion

Both platforms can be profitable, but YouTube generally offers higher long-term RPM.
