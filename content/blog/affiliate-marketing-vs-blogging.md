# Affiliate Marketing vs Blogging

Many beginners wonder whether they should focus on blogging or affiliate marketing.

The truth is that they often work together.

## Blogging

A blog is a platform used to publish content.

Advantages:

* SEO traffic
* Long-term growth
* Audience ownership

## Affiliate Marketing

Affiliate marketing is a monetization method.

Advantages:

* No product creation
* Scalable income
* Passive revenue potential

## The Best Approach

Most successful publishers combine both:

Blog → Traffic

Affiliate Links → Revenue

## Example

A creator publishes:

* Product reviews
* Tutorials
* Comparisons

The content attracts traffic and affiliate links generate commissions.

## Final Thoughts

Blogging and affiliate marketing are not competitors. They are often most effective when combined.
