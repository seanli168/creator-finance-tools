# What Is A Good TikTok RPM?

TikTok RPM varies depending on niche and audience.

## Average RPM

Many creators see:

* $0.20–$1 RPM

## Good RPM

A good RPM is often:

* $1–$3 RPM

## Excellent RPM

High-performing creators may earn:

* $3–$10+ RPM

## Why RPM Differs

Factors include:

* Audience country
* Engagement
* Watch time
* Niche

## Final Thoughts

A good TikTok RPM depends on your niche, but anything above $1 RPM is generally considered strong.
