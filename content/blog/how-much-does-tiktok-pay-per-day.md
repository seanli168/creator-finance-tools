# How Much Does TikTok Pay Per Day?

There is no fixed amount TikTok pays creators per day.

## Daily Earnings Depend On

* Daily views
* RPM
* Audience location
* Content performance

## Example

If a creator receives:

100,000 views per day

RPM = $2

Daily earnings may be:

$200

## Other Revenue Sources

Many creators also earn from:

* Sponsorships
* Affiliate commissions
* Product sales

## Final Thoughts

Daily income can fluctuate significantly based on content performance and audience quality.
