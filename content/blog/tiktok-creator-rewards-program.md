# TikTok Creator Rewards Program

The Creator Rewards Program is TikTok's monetization system for eligible creators.

## Requirements

* Eligible country
* Minimum follower count
* Content guidelines compliance

## How Earnings Work

Revenue is based on:

* Views
* Engagement
* Watch time
* Audience quality

## Tips For Success

* Publish original content
* Maintain high retention
* Focus on audience engagement

## Final Thoughts

The Creator Rewards Program provides a direct monetization path for growing creators.
