# What Is TikTok RPM?

TikTok RPM is one of the most important metrics for creators who want to understand their earnings.

RPM stands for **Revenue Per Mille**, which means the amount of money a creator earns for every 1,000 views.

Understanding RPM helps creators estimate potential revenue and optimize their content strategy.

## How Is TikTok RPM Calculated?

The formula is:

RPM = Revenue ÷ Views × 1000

For example:

* Revenue: $100
* Views: 50,000

RPM = $100 ÷ 50,000 × 1000

RPM = $2

This means the creator earns $2 for every 1,000 views.

## What Is A Good TikTok RPM?

Typical TikTok RPM ranges:

* Low RPM: $0.20 – $1
* Average RPM: $1 – $4
* High RPM: $4 – $10+

The actual RPM depends on niche, audience location, engagement, and advertiser demand.

## Factors That Affect TikTok RPM

Several factors influence RPM:

* Audience country
* Content niche
* Watch time
* Engagement rate
* Advertiser demand
* Content quality

Creators targeting audiences in the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom often see higher RPM.

## Highest Paying TikTok Niches

Examples include:

* Finance
* Investing
* Business
* Technology
* Software
* Personal Development

These categories attract premium advertisers.

## Lowest Paying Niches

Common lower-RPM categories include:

* Memes
* Entertainment clips
* General humor
* Viral repost content

## How To Increase TikTok RPM

Creators can improve RPM by:

* Targeting higher-value audiences
* Creating advertiser-friendly content
* Improving watch time
* Publishing consistently
* Focusing on profitable niches

## Final Thoughts

TikTok RPM is a valuable metric that helps creators understand how much money they earn from views.

While RPM varies widely between niches and countries, creators who focus on quality content and valuable audiences can significantly increase their earnings.
