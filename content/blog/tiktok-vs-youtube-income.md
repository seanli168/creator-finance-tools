# TikTok vs YouTube Income

Creators often compare TikTok and YouTube monetization.

## Revenue Comparison

| Platform | RPM            | Stability |
| -------- | -------------- | --------- |
| TikTok   | Low to Medium  | Low       |
| YouTube  | Medium to High | High      |

## TikTok Pros

* Fast growth
* Viral potential
* Brand deals

## YouTube Pros

* Higher RPM
* Long-term income
* Search traffic

## Best Strategy

Many creators use both platforms:

* TikTok → traffic
* YouTube → monetization

## Final Thoughts

YouTube is better for long-term income, while TikTok is better for growth.
