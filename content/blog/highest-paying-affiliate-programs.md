# Highest Paying Affiliate Programs

Not all affiliate programs pay the same.

Some programs offer commissions worth hundreds or even thousands of dollars per sale.

## High-Paying Categories

### Software

Often provides recurring commissions.

### Finance

Credit cards, loans, and investing services can pay significant commissions.

### Web Hosting

Many hosting companies offer generous payouts.

### Online Education

Courses and coaching programs frequently provide high commission rates.

## Factors To Consider

Don't focus only on commission size.

Also evaluate:

* Conversion rate
* Brand trust
* Refund rate
* Customer demand

## Final Thoughts

The best affiliate program combines strong commissions with products people genuinely want.
