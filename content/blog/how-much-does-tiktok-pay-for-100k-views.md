# How Much Does TikTok Pay For 100K Views?

TikTok earnings depend on RPM rather than view count alone.

## Example Earnings

### RPM = $1

100,000 views = $100

### RPM = $3

100,000 views = $300

### RPM = $5

100,000 views = $500

## Why Earnings Differ

Factors include:

* Country
* Niche
* Watch time
* Engagement

## Final Thoughts

100K views can generate very different amounts depending on audience quality.
