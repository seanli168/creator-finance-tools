# Highest RPM Niches On YouTube

The highest RPM niches on YouTube are usually connected to industries with expensive products and strong advertiser competition.

---

# Top High RPM Niches

| Niche | Typical RPM |
|---|---|
| Finance | $15-$40 |
| Business | $10-$30 |
| Software | $8-$25 |
| Real Estate | $10-$35 |
| Insurance | $15-$50 |

---

# Why Finance Has High RPM

Financial advertisers compete heavily for customers.

Credit cards, loans, and investing platforms generate large profits per customer.

This allows advertisers to pay more for YouTube ads.

---

# How To Enter High RPM Niches

Creators should focus on:

- Evergreen topics
- Search traffic
- Long-form educational content
