# How To Increase TikTok RPM

Increasing RPM can dramatically improve creator income.

## Focus On High Value Niches

* Finance
* Technology
* Business
* Software

## Target Premium Countries

Audiences in the USA, UK, Canada, and Australia typically generate higher RPM.

## Improve Watch Time

Longer watch sessions often lead to better monetization performance.

## Create Advertiser-Friendly Content

Avoid controversial topics that reduce advertiser demand.

## Final Thoughts

RPM growth often comes from better audiences and stronger content quality.
