# How Much Do Finance YouTubers Make?

Finance creators often earn more than creators in entertainment niches.

## Small Channels

Many earn:

* $100-$1,000/month

## Medium Channels

Channels with consistent traffic may earn:

* $1,000-$10,000/month

## Large Channels

Successful finance channels can generate:

* $10,000+
* $50,000+
* Six figures per month

## Multiple Revenue Streams

Most finance creators combine:

* Ad revenue
* Affiliate marketing
* Sponsorships
* Digital products

## Final Thoughts

Finance YouTubers benefit from some of the highest RPMs available on the platform.
