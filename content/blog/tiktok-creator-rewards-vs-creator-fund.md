# TikTok Creator Rewards vs Creator Fund

TikTok replaced the Creator Fund with the Creator Rewards Program.

## Creator Fund

The original monetization system.

Many creators reported relatively low earnings.

## Creator Rewards Program

Designed to reward higher-quality content.

Benefits include:

* Potentially higher RPM
* Better monetization opportunities
* Focus on engagement and watch time

## Key Differences

| Feature            | Creator Fund | Creator Rewards        |
| ------------------ | ------------ | ---------------------- |
| Earnings Potential | Lower        | Higher                 |
| Content Focus      | General      | Higher-quality content |
| RPM Potential      | Lower        | Higher                 |

## Final Thoughts

Many creators have reported stronger earnings under the Creator Rewards Program.
