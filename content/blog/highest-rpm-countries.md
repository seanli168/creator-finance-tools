# Highest RPM Countries For Creators

Audience location is one of the biggest factors affecting RPM.

Some countries consistently generate significantly higher advertising revenue.

## Top RPM Countries

### United States

The US remains the highest-value advertising market.

### Canada

Strong advertiser demand and high purchasing power.

### United Kingdom

One of the most profitable English-speaking markets.

### Australia

Premium advertisers and strong CPM rates.

### Germany

High-value European audience.

## Why Country Matters

Advertisers pay more in countries where:

* Consumer spending is high
* Competition is strong
* Online purchases are common

## Final Thoughts

Creators targeting high-value countries often earn significantly more revenue per view.
