# Can You Monetize YouTube With 500 Subscribers?

Many new creators reach 500 subscribers and wonder whether monetization is possible.

## Can You Earn Ad Revenue?

In most cases:

No.

Traditional YouTube ad revenue requires higher thresholds.

## What Can You Do With 500 Subscribers?

You can still earn money through:

* Affiliate marketing
* Sponsorships
* Digital products
* Coaching services

## Why 500 Subscribers Is Important

Reaching 500 subscribers shows that your channel is growing and building an audience.

This is often the stage where creators begin experimenting with monetization.

## Final Thoughts

While 500 subscribers is not enough for full YouTube ad monetization, there are still several ways to generate income.
