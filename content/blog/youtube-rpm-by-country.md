# YouTube RPM By Country

YouTube RPM varies significantly depending on audience location.

Some countries generate much higher advertiser spending than others.

---

# Average RPM By Country

| Country | Estimated RPM |
|---|---|
| United States | $8-$30 |
| Canada | $6-$20 |
| United Kingdom | $5-$18 |
| Australia | $5-$15 |
| Germany | $4-$12 |
| India | $0.5-$3 |

These numbers vary by niche and season.

---

# Why Country Matters

Advertisers in developed countries spend more money to acquire customers.

This increases CPM and ultimately creator RPM.

---

# Best Countries For YouTube Revenue

The highest RPM countries usually include:

- United States
- Canada
- United Kingdom
- Australia
- Switzerland

Finance and business audiences in these countries often produce extremely high RPM.

---

# How To Target Higher RPM Countries

Creators can improve RPM by:

- Publishing English content
- Covering global topics
- Creating search-based videos
- Targeting US audiences

---

# Final Thoughts

Audience geography is one of the biggest factors affecting YouTube revenue.

Even small channels can generate strong earnings with high-quality international audiences.
