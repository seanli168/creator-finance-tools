# Best CPM Countries For YouTube

CPM stands for Cost Per Mille.

It measures how much advertisers pay for 1,000 ad impressions.

Some countries consistently generate much higher CPM than others.

---

# Highest CPM Countries

| Country | Estimated CPM |
|---|---|
| United States | $15-$50 |
| Canada | $10-$35 |
| United Kingdom | $8-$30 |
| Australia | $8-$25 |
| Germany | $6-$20 |

Finance and software channels can generate even higher CPM rates.

---

# Why CPM Is Higher In Some Countries

Advertisers in developed countries often have larger budgets.

Customer lifetime value is also much higher.

This allows companies to spend more money on YouTube advertising.

---

# Niches With The Highest CPM

High CPM industries include:

- Finance
- Insurance
- Real estate
- SaaS
- Business

---

# CPM vs RPM

CPM measures advertiser spending.

RPM measures creator earnings after YouTube's revenue share.

RPM is always lower than CPM.

---

# Final Thoughts

Country targeting is one of the easiest ways to improve YouTube monetization.

Channels focused on high-value audiences often earn dramatically more revenue.
