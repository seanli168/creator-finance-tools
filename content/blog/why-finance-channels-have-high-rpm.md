# Why Finance Channels Have High RPM

If you’ve spent time researching YouTube monetization, you’ve probably heard people say:

“Finance channels have the highest RPM.”

That’s usually true.

Some finance creators earn over $20, $30, or even $50 RPM.

But why?

---

# Advertisers Pay More For Financial Customers

The answer is simple:

Financial customers are extremely valuable.

Think about products like:

- Credit cards
- Investing apps
- Insurance
- Loans
- Trading platforms

A single customer can generate hundreds or even thousands of dollars for a company.

Because of that, advertisers are willing to spend aggressively.

---

# Finance Audiences Are Valuable

Finance viewers are often:

- Adults
- Professionals
- Business owners
- Investors

These audiences usually have stronger buying power.

Advertisers love that.

---

# CPM Competition Is Extremely High

Finance is one of the most competitive advertising industries online.

Multiple companies constantly compete for the same audience.

That drives CPM higher.

Higher CPM usually leads to higher RPM for creators.

---

# Search-Based Finance Content Performs Well

Many finance videos are evergreen.

Examples include:

- How to invest
- Best credit cards
- Budgeting tips
- Side hustle ideas

These topics continue getting search traffic for years.

That creates stable long-term revenue.

---

# Finance Channels Also Make Money Outside Ads

A lot of finance creators earn more from:

- Affiliate marketing
- Sponsorships
- Courses
- Newsletters

Ad revenue is often only one part of the business.

---

# Is Finance Too Competitive?

It’s competitive, yes.

But smaller creators can still succeed by targeting specific subtopics.

For example:

- Student budgeting
- Beginner investing
- Freelance finance
- Creator business tips

Niche focus matters a lot.
