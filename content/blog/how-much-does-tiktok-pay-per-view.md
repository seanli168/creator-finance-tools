# How Much Does TikTok Pay Per View?

TikTok pays creators based on RPM, not fixed per-view rates.

## Average TikTok Earnings

* $0.02 – $0.05 per 1000 views (low)
* $0.20 – $2 per 1000 views (average)
* $5+ per 1000 views (high RPM accounts)

## Why TikTok Pays Less Than YouTube

* Shorter content
* Less ad inventory
* Lower advertiser competition

## How To Increase Earnings

* Target US audience
* Focus on high RPM niches
* Improve watch time

## Final Thoughts

TikTok income depends heavily on audience quality.
