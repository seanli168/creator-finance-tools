# Can Affiliate Marketing Make You Rich?

Yes, affiliate marketing can make you rich—but only under specific conditions.

## What Top Affiliates Earn

* $1,000/month beginners
* $10,000/month intermediate
* $100,000+/month top affiliates

## What You Need To Succeed

### 1. Traffic

Without traffic, no income.

### 2. High-paying niche

Software, finance, AI tools.

### 3. Trust

Audience trust increases conversions.

## Common Misconceptions

* Not passive at the beginning
* Requires consistent content
* Needs SEO or traffic source

## Final Thoughts

Affiliate marketing can create wealth, but it is not a quick scheme.
