# YouTube RPM By Country

RPM varies dramatically between countries.

## Typical RPM Ranges

| Country        | RPM Range |
| -------------- | --------- |
| United States  | $5-$25+   |
| Canada         | $4-$20    |
| United Kingdom | $4-$20    |
| Australia      | $4-$18    |
| Germany        | $3-$15    |
| India          | $0.5-$3   |

## Why RPM Differs

Factors include:

* Advertising demand
* Audience purchasing power
* Competition among advertisers

## How Creators Benefit

Creators who attract audiences from premium countries often earn more revenue from the same number of views.

## Final Thoughts

Geographic audience composition can have a major impact on YouTube earnings.
