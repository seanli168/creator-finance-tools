# Why Is My TikTok RPM So Low?

Many creators notice that their RPM seems lower than expected.

## Common Reasons

### Low Watch Time

Short watch duration can reduce content value.

### Low Audience Quality

Some regions generate lower advertiser demand.

### Weak Engagement

Videos with poor engagement may receive fewer recommendations.

### Niche Selection

Some niches naturally have lower advertising budgets.

## How To Improve RPM

* Improve watch time
* Create higher-value content
* Target premium audiences
* Increase engagement

## Final Thoughts

Low RPM is usually caused by audience quality, watch time, and content category rather than a technical problem.
