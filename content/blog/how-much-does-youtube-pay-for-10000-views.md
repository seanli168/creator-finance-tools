# How Much Does YouTube Pay For 10,000 Views?

For new creators, 10,000 views often feels like a major milestone.

But how much money does that actually generate?

---

# Example Revenue

| RPM | Estimated Earnings |
|---|---|
| $1 | $10 |
| $3 | $30 |
| $5 | $50 |

Actual earnings may vary.

---

# RPM Makes The Difference

A creator in a finance niche may earn several times more than a creator in a gaming niche with the same number of views.

---

# Views Alone Don't Tell The Full Story

Advertisers care about:

- Audience demographics
- Location
- Buying intent

That's why some channels earn much more per view.

---

# Monetization Beyond Ads

Many creators also earn from:

- Sponsorships
- Affiliate marketing
- Digital products

These revenue streams often exceed ad revenue.

---

# Final Thoughts

10,000 views is a great milestone, but earnings depend heavily on RPM and audience quality.
