# YouTube RPM For Gaming Channels

Gaming remains one of the most popular content categories on YouTube.

Millions of creators publish gaming videos every day.

---

# Is Gaming RPM High?

Generally, gaming RPM is lower than:

- Finance
- Business
- Software

However, gaming RPM is usually higher than many viral entertainment categories.

---

# Typical Gaming RPM

Many gaming creators report RPM values between:

```text
$1 - $5
```

although results vary by audience and content type.

---

# Audience Location Matters

Gaming channels targeting:

- United States
- Canada
- Australia

often earn more than channels focused on lower-income regions.

---

# Long Videos Help

Long-form gameplay videos can often include more ads.

This usually increases RPM.

---

# Sponsorships Matter

Gaming creators often earn significant income through:

- Hardware partnerships
- Game sponsorships
- Affiliate marketing

For larger creators, sponsorships may exceed ad revenue.

---

# Final Thoughts

Gaming RPM is usually lower than finance or software content, but gaming remains one of the largest and most accessible YouTube niches.
