# Best SaaS Affiliate Programs

Software affiliate programs are among the highest-paying affiliate opportunities online.

## Why SaaS Programs Are Attractive

Benefits include:

* High commissions
* Recurring revenue
* Strong customer lifetime value

## Popular SaaS Affiliate Programs

### Shopify

One of the largest ecommerce platforms.

### Semrush

Popular SEO software.

### HubSpot

CRM and marketing software.

### ConvertKit

Email marketing platform.

### Notion

Productivity software.

## What Makes A Great SaaS Program?

Look for:

* Recurring commissions
* Long cookie duration
* Reliable tracking
* Strong brand reputation

## Final Thoughts

SaaS affiliate programs often provide some of the highest long-term affiliate income opportunities.
