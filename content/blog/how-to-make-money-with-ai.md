# How To Make Money With AI

Artificial intelligence can help creators and entrepreneurs build new income streams.

## Affiliate Marketing

Promote AI tools and software products.

## Content Creation

Use AI to assist with:

* Blogs
* YouTube scripts
* Social media posts

## Digital Products

Create:

* Templates
* Guides
* Courses

## Freelance Services

Offer AI-assisted services such as writing and design.

## Software Businesses

Some entrepreneurs build niche AI tools and subscription services.

## Final Thoughts

AI is not a shortcut to wealth, but it can significantly improve productivity and income potential.
