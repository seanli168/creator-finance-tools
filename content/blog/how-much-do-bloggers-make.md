# How Much Do Bloggers Make?

Blogging income varies dramatically.

Some blogs earn a few dollars per month while others generate six or seven figures annually.

## Beginner Bloggers

Most new bloggers earn:

* $0 to $100 per month
* $100 to $500 per month

The first year is often focused on content creation and SEO.

## Intermediate Bloggers

Established blogs often generate:

* $500 to $5,000 per month

At this stage, search traffic begins producing meaningful revenue.

## Professional Bloggers

Large authority sites may earn:

* $10,000+
* $50,000+
* $100,000+ monthly

## Main Revenue Sources

* Display advertising
* Affiliate marketing
* Sponsored content
* Digital products
* Memberships

## Final Thoughts

Blogging income depends heavily on traffic, niche selection, and monetization strategy.
