# How Much Does YouTube Pay For 100K Views?

Many creators reach their first viral video and wonder:

**How much does YouTube pay for 100K views?**

The answer depends on RPM.

## Example Earnings

| RPM | Earnings For 100K Views |
| --- | ----------------------- |
| $1  | $100                    |
| $3  | $300                    |
| $5  | $500                    |
| $10 | $1,000                  |
| $20 | $2,000                  |

## Why RPM Matters

RPM determines creator revenue.

Two channels with the same views may earn dramatically different amounts.

A finance channel often earns significantly more than an entertainment channel.

## Factors Affecting Revenue

* Niche
* Audience location
* Watch time
* Advertiser demand
* Seasonality

## High-RPM Niches

* Finance
* Business
* Software
* Real Estate

## Conclusion

100,000 views can generate anywhere from $100 to more than $2,000 depending on RPM.
