# YouTube RPM For Finance Channels

Finance content consistently ranks among the highest-paying YouTube categories.

## Why Finance RPM Is High

Financial advertisers are willing to spend more because:

* Customers are valuable
* Products generate recurring revenue
* Competition is strong

## Common Finance Advertisers

* Banks
* Credit card companies
* Investment platforms
* Insurance providers

## Typical RPM Range

Many finance channels report RPM values between:

$10 and $40+

Some premium finance audiences may exceed this range.

## Examples

Topics include:

* Investing
* Stock market
* Retirement planning
* Side hustles

## Final Thoughts

Finance remains one of the most profitable YouTube niches available today.
