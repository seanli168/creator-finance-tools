# YouTube RPM For Beginners

If you're new to YouTube monetization, RPM is one of the most important metrics to understand.

## What Does RPM Mean?

RPM stands for Revenue Per Mille.

It shows how much money you earn per 1,000 views.

## Why RPM Matters

RPM gives creators a realistic picture of earnings.

Unlike CPM, RPM includes YouTube's revenue share.

## Example

* 10,000 views
* RPM = $5

Estimated earnings:

$50

## Final Thoughts

Learning RPM early helps creators make smarter content decisions.
