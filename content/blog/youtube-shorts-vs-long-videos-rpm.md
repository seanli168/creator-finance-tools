# YouTube Shorts vs Long Videos RPM: Which Pays More?

Many YouTube creators wonder whether Shorts or long-form videos generate more revenue.

Both formats can help creators grow their channels, but their RPM (Revenue Per Mille) can be very different.

Understanding the difference between YouTube Shorts RPM and long-form video RPM can help creators choose a better content strategy.

---

# What Is YouTube RPM?

RPM means:

**Revenue Per Mille**

It represents how much money a creator earns per 1,000 views after YouTube's revenue share.

YouTube RPM depends on:

- Audience country
- Content niche
- Viewer engagement
- Video length
- Advertiser demand

---

# YouTube Shorts RPM vs Long Videos RPM

The biggest difference between Shorts and long videos is advertising.

## RPM Comparison

| Content Type | Average RPM Range |
|---|---|
| YouTube Shorts | $0.01 - $0.10 |
| Long-form Videos | $2 - $15+ |

Long-form videos usually generate significantly higher RPM.

---

# Why YouTube Long Videos Usually Have Higher RPM

Long videos typically earn more because they provide more advertising opportunities.

## 1. More Ad Placements

Long-form videos can include:

- Pre-roll ads
- Mid-roll ads
- Display ads

Shorts use a different revenue-sharing model.

---

## 2. Higher Viewer Intent

Long-form viewers often spend more time watching content.

Examples:

- Product reviews
- Tutorials
- Educational videos
- Finance videos

These audiences are more valuable to advertisers.

---

## 3. Better Monetization Niches

Many high RPM niches are based on long-form content.

Examples:

- Finance
- Technology
- Software
- Business
- Education

---

# How Much Do YouTube Shorts Pay?

Shorts can generate revenue, but RPM is usually much lower.

Example:

## 1 Million Shorts Views

Estimated earnings:

$10 - $100+

Actual earnings depend on:

- Audience location
- Engagement
- Content category

# How Much Do Long Videos Pay?

Long-form videos usually have stronger earning potential.

Example:

## 1 Million Long-Form Views

Possible earnings:

$2,000 - $10,000+

High-value niches may generate even more.

Although Shorts RPM is lower, Shorts have important benefits.

## Faster Growth

## More Subscribers

## Content Testing

Creators can test ideas before creating longer videos.

# YouTube Shorts Disadvantages

Shorts also have limitations.

- Watch longer content
