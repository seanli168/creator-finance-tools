# Best YouTube RPM Niches

Not all YouTube niches earn the same RPM.

Some topics attract premium advertisers and significantly higher earnings.

## 1. Personal Finance

Finance is consistently one of the highest-paying YouTube categories.

Topics include:

* Investing
* Credit cards
* Budgeting
* Retirement planning

## 2. Business

Business-related audiences are highly valuable to advertisers.

## 3. Software Reviews

SaaS companies often pay premium advertising rates.

## 4. AI Tools

Artificial intelligence continues to attract high advertiser demand.

## 5. Online Marketing

Marketing software and services often have strong CPMs.

## Average RPM Comparison

| Niche         | RPM Range |
| ------------- | --------- |
| Entertainment | $1-$4     |
| Gaming        | $2-$6     |
| Tech          | $4-$10    |
| Finance       | $10-$30+  |

## Final Thoughts

Choosing the right niche can have a larger impact on earnings than increasing views.
