# ClickBank vs Amazon Associates

Two of the most popular affiliate networks are ClickBank and Amazon Associates.

## ClickBank

Advantages:

* High commissions
* Digital products
* Global availability

Disadvantages:

* Product quality varies
* Some niches are highly competitive

## Amazon Associates

Advantages:

* Massive product catalog
* Strong brand trust
* High conversion rates

Disadvantages:

* Lower commission rates
* Short cookie duration

## Which Is Better?

Choose Amazon if:

* You review physical products
* You want higher conversion rates

Choose ClickBank if:

* You promote digital products
* You want larger commissions

## Final Thoughts

Both platforms can be profitable. The best choice depends on your niche and audience.
