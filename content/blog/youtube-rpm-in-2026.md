# YouTube RPM in 2026

YouTube RPM continues to evolve as advertising demand and viewer behavior change.

## What Is RPM?

RPM (Revenue Per Mille) is the revenue earned per 1,000 views after YouTube’s revenue share.

## Expected RPM Range in 2026

* Low RPM: $1 - $3
* Average RPM: $3 - $8
* High RPM: $8 - $20+

## What Is Changing in 2026?

* More AI-driven ad targeting
* Higher competition for finance niches
* Increased mobile ad inventory
* Growth in Shorts monetization

## Best Niches for High RPM

* Finance
* SaaS
* Tech reviews
* Business education

## Final Thoughts

YouTube RPM in 2026 is expected to favor high-value niches even more than before.
