# YouTube Monetization Requirements 2026

Many creators ask the same question:

What do you need to qualify for YouTube monetization?

## YouTube Partner Program Requirements

Creators generally need one of the following:

### Option 1

* 1,000 subscribers
* 4,000 public watch hours in the past 12 months

### Option 2

* 1,000 subscribers
* 10 million valid Shorts views in the last 90 days

## Additional Requirements

Creators must also:

* Follow YouTube policies
* Enable two-factor authentication
* Have no active Community Guidelines strikes

## How Long Does Approval Take?

Most applications are reviewed within a few weeks.

Approval time depends on:

* Channel quality
* Policy compliance
* Review volume

## Final Thoughts

The fastest way to qualify is to consistently publish high-quality content and focus on audience retention.
