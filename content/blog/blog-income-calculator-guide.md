# Blog Income Calculator Guide

A blog income calculator helps estimate earnings based on traffic and RPM.

## Basic Formula

Revenue = Pageviews × RPM ÷ 1000

## Example

* Monthly Pageviews: 100,000
* RPM: $15

Estimated Revenue:

$1,500/month

## What Affects Blog RPM?

* Traffic location
* Content niche
* Ad placement
* User engagement

## High RPM Niches

* Finance
* Insurance
* Software
* Business

## Final Thoughts

Income calculators provide useful estimates but actual results vary by website.
