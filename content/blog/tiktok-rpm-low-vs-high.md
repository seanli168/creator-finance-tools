# TikTok RPM Low vs High

TikTok RPM can vary dramatically between creators.

## Low RPM Causes

* Low-value audience countries
* Entertainment content
* Low engagement
* Reposted content

## High RPM Causes

* Finance or business niche
* US/UK audience
* High watch time
* Original content

## Example

* Low RPM: $0.30 per 1,000 views
* High RPM: $5–$10 per 1,000 views

## Final Thoughts

The difference in RPM is mainly driven by audience quality and niche selection.
