# TikTok RPM By Country

TikTok RPM varies significantly depending on audience location.

## Highest RPM Countries

* United States
* Canada
* United Kingdom
* Australia
* Germany

## Why Country Matters

Advertisers pay more in countries with stronger purchasing power and higher competition.
