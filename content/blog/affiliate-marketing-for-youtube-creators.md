# Affiliate Marketing For YouTube Creators

Affiliate marketing is one of the most powerful income streams for YouTubers.

## How It Works

Creators add affiliate links in:

* Video descriptions
* Pinned comments
* Reviews

## Best Video Types

* Product reviews
* Tutorials
* Comparisons
* Top 10 lists

## High-Converting Niches

* Tech reviews
* Software tools
* Finance products
* Online courses

## Final Thoughts

Affiliate marketing can significantly increase YouTube earnings when combined with RPM revenue.
