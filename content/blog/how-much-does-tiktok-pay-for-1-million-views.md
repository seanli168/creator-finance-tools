# How Much Does TikTok Pay For 1 Million Views?

Many creators wonder how much money 1 million TikTok views can generate.

## Typical Earnings

Depending on RPM:

* $0.20 RPM = $200
* $1 RPM = $1,000
* $3 RPM = $3,000

## What Affects Earnings?

### Audience Location

US audiences generally generate higher RPM.

### Engagement

Strong engagement can improve monetization opportunities.

### Watch Time

Longer watch times often correlate with better earnings.

## Final Thoughts

There is no fixed payment amount for 1 million views. RPM determines actual earnings.
