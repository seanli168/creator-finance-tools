# How Much Does YouTube Pay For Shorts?

YouTube Shorts has become one of the fastest ways for creators to grow an audience.

But many creators ask:

**How much money does YouTube pay for Shorts views?**

The answer depends on:

- Shorts RPM
- Audience location
- Number of views
- Engagement
- Content category

Compared with traditional YouTube videos, Shorts usually have lower RPM, but they can generate millions of views quickly.

---

# How Does YouTube Shorts Monetization Work?

YouTube Shorts monetization works differently from regular videos.

Instead of placing ads directly inside each Short, YouTube displays ads between Shorts in the Shorts feed.

The advertising revenue is collected into a shared pool.

Creators receive a portion of that revenue based on their eligible views.

# Why Is Shorts RPM Lower Than Long Videos?

Shorts usually have lower RPM because:

## Shorter Viewing Time

Shorts are designed for quick consumption.

Viewers spend less time with each piece of content.

---

## Different Advertising Model

Long videos can include:

- Pre-roll ads
- Mid-roll ads
- Display ads

Shorts use a shared advertising pool.

---

## Lower Buyer Intent

Long-form viewers are often searching for:

- Reviews
- Tutorials
- Product information

These audiences are more valuable to advertisers.

- Watch time
