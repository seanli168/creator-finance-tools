# How Affiliate Marketing Works

Affiliate marketing is a performance-based business model where affiliates earn commissions by promoting products or services.

## The Basic Process

The affiliate marketing process includes:

1. Joining an affiliate program
2. Receiving a unique tracking link
3. Promoting products
4. Generating sales
5. Receiving commissions

## Affiliate Tracking

Each affiliate receives a unique referral link.

When a visitor clicks the link, a tracking cookie records the referral.

If the visitor makes a purchase, the affiliate receives a commission.

## Common Commission Structures

### Percentage-Based

Example:

* Product price: $100
* Commission rate: 10%
* Earnings: $10

### Fixed Commission

Example:

* Software signup
* Commission: $50 per customer

## Why Companies Use Affiliate Marketing

Businesses benefit because they only pay when results occur.

This makes affiliate marketing one of the most efficient customer acquisition channels.

## Final Thoughts

Affiliate marketing creates a win-win relationship between businesses and content creators.
